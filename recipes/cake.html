Cake

This page will teach you how to make a simple cake

This recipe is amazingly simple and only requires 9 count them 9 items and your handy dandy crafting table!

Ingredients

  • Three buckets of milk
  • Two sugar
  • One egg
  • Three wheat

Steps

  1. First Step
    1. Acquire required ingredients
    2. Build a crafting table from four wood
  2. Second Step
    1. Place your three buckets of milk in the three slots along the top row
    2. Place your egg in the middle
    3. Place your three wheat across the bottom three slots
    4. Place the remaining sugar in the last two slots
  3. Ta Da! You have yourself a delicious cake!
