Mushroom Stew

This page will teach you how to make delectable Mushroom Stew

This recipe is amazingly simple and only requires 3 count them 3 items and your handy dandy crafting table!

Ingredients

  • One Red Mushroom
  • One Brown Mushroom
  • One Bowl

Steps

  1. First Step
    1. Acquire required ingredients
    2. Build a crafting table from four wood
  2. Second Step
    1. Place your Red Mushroom in the middle left slot
    2. Place your Brown Mushroom in the very middle slot
    3. Place your Bowl in the slot under the Brown Mushroom
  3. Ta Da! You have yourself a delicious cake!
