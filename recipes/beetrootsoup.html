Beetroot Soup

This page will teach you how to make Beetroot Soup

This recipe is amazingly simple and only requires 7 count them 9 items and your handy dandy crafting table!

Ingredients

  • Six Beetroots
  • One Bowl

Steps

  1. First Step
    1. Acquire required ingredients
    2. Build a crafting table from four wood
  2. Second Step
    1. Place your six beetroots such that they fill up the first two rows of the crafting table
    2. Place your bowl in the bottom middle slot
  3. Ta Da! You have yourself a delicious cake!
